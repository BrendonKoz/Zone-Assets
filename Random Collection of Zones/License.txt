Welcome to Infantry, Soldier!

Infantry Online is a multiplayer combat video game with sprite animation graphics, using complex soldier, ground vehicle and space-ship models on typically complex terrains. Team with gamers from around the world in this adrenaline-pumping collection of online combat games. It's all-out frenetic action in the world of Infantry where battles featuring up to 100+ wage throughout the day. Easy to play and simple to get into, Infantry offers a quick blend of tactics and action unlike any other massively multiplayer online game.

There is no license... just play!
